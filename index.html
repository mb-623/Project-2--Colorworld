Dedicated to Creativity

At this resort, we are not just looking to provide a night away from home, but a whole other world of creativity for the young and young at heart. Our goal is for everyone to express themselves artistically for the world to see, and with our activities we hope that this overnight trip becomes a once in a lifetime immersive experience.

About Us

Built in 2022, we want to help inspire young minds in all aspects of life. We designed this hotel with this goal in mind, allowing creativity to be expressed in all formats during a night stay here. Nobody leaves uninspired!

A location for you
and your family

Our resort is conveniently located nearby the Crayola Experience in Pennsylvania. To accommodate such an attraction, we offer travel to and from the location as an amenity. Additionally, we work hard to provide additional experiences within our doors to continue inspiring creative minds after their visit is over.

Services for a Fun Day

Our hotel provides busing services complimentary with your stay, helping to save you from the struggle of finding parking for an incredible experience!
